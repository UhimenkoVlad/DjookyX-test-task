Welcome to DjookyX

DjookyX is the world’s first music investment and trading online platform, a marketplace where artists can sell intellectual property (IP) rights in their music to fans and investors who, in return, will benefit jointly from the potential future income generated by the song(s) they support.

How does it work?

Artists

  1. Upload your song(s) to the marketplace
  2. Decide how many IP rights you would like to sell
  3. Set the minimum price per IP right
  4. Set up a start date for the count down
  5. Agree to the T&Cs for the initial sale and transfer of the song rights

Investors

  1. Buy IP rights in the song of your favourite artist
  2. Sell at any time and transfer your IP rights to other investors if you decide to do so
  3. Enjoy high security of transactions and liquidity of your assets

Why should I sign up?

DjookyX for Artists

  • Access the global investor community
  • Generate the funds needed to develop and accelerate your music career
  • Retain your freedom while sharing the royalties with the community of music fans who have supported you on your way to success

DjookyX for Investors

  • For the first time in history you have the opportunity to invest and trade in music as a new and exciting asset class
  • Together with the Artists you will benefit from the royalties a song may be generating in the future
  • Become an ethical investor supporting the new generation of creators from across the world

Security

We use sophisticated technology and embedded procedures to ensure the highest security levels for every transaction.

Our public register of the owners of music rights is supported by the blockchain technology ensuring legal compliance as well as accuracy, transparency, and real-time record of rights ownership.
